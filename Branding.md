# Branding · Type Directions

Premium, timeless options (license required):
- Primary sans: **Söhne** (Klim). Modern grotesk with subtle character; ideal for UI, headings, long text.
- Alternate sans: **Neue Haas Grotesk** (Linotype) or **Suisse Intl** (Swiss Typefaces) for a cooler editorial feel.
- Accent serif: **Canela** (Commercial Type) for luxe headlines; alternates: **Domaine**, **GT Sectra**, or **Suisse Works** for sharper editorial tone.
- Suggested pairing: **Söhne + Canela** (top pick) or **Suisse Intl + Suisse Works** for a unified family system.

Open-license preview/stand-ins (no cost):
- Primary sans: **Space Grotesk** or **Inter**.
- Accent serif: **Libre Baskerville** or **EB Garamond**.
- Pairing suggestion: Space Grotesk + Libre Baskerville (if contrast is desired) or Space Grotesk alone for minimal contrast.

Notes:
- Goal: timeless, high-fashion “ruler/magician” tone; avoid trendy faces.
- If licensing premiums, self-host via @font-face and set font-display: swap.
- Keep tracking tight on headings; generous leading on body for readability.
